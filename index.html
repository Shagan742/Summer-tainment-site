My Experience At Costco

About

Costco is a wholesale store I like to shop at for groceries that can feed my whole family. Since this wholesale store sells items in larger quantities, they last longer and cost fewer grocery trips as well. Everything here is family sized.

Food

Ice Creams

Chocolate Sundae

This sundae is basically like an unmixed chocolate ice cream. The cup is a huge soft serve with chocolate syrup on top. Personally, I find chocolate to be a bit too sweet for me, so this is not bad but also not my favorite.

Quality: ⭐⭐⭐

Vanilla Ice Cream

This flavor is a good one to have if you don't feel a need for a fancy schmancy sundae. However, even though I like vanilla, it tastes kind of plain for me. The ice cream is soft and tastes good though.

Quality: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Strawberry Sundae

The strawberry sundae is basically vanilla ice cream but with strawberries and syrup on it. I find this one to be my favorite out of the 3 because of the fruitiness the strawberry flavor brings.

Quality: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Other Foods I had

Chicken Bake

The chicken bake is a long stick mostly made of chicken and bacon. It tastes pretty good, however, the inside was way too hot for me. I kind of burned my tongue with this, but it was not really that bad.

Quality: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Cheese Pizza

This is by far the only pizza I have had, and it slaps. Even though this pizza is really good, I'm not a sauce person, so that loses a star. Another thing I'm not fond of is when the cheese gets harder once the pizza cools down.

Quality: ⭐⭐⭐
